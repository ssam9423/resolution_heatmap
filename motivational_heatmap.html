About

This webpage is to keep track of the user's 2025 New Years Resolutions.
It will show heatmaps for motivation, and include links to aid in completing the user's goals.
The user will be able to toggle each day as complete or incomplete (Boolean).
The user will also be able to record any hours spent or notes to show up when hovering over a completed day.
